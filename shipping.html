Shipping

Shipping Information – Glow Haus Delivers to Your Door! Whether you're in Ho Chi Minh City or halfway across the country, we make sure your favorite Glow Haus products get to you — safely, quickly, and beautifully packed. Here’s everything you need to know about how we ship:

📦 Domestic Shipping (Vietnam-Wide)

We currently offer shipping to all 63 provinces in Vietnam, including remote areas. All orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation (excluding Sundays & public holidays). You'll receive a tracking number via SMS or email as soon as your order is on its way.

Delivery Options:

– Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery in 2–5 business days, depending on your location.

Express Shipping:

Available in major cities like HCMC, Hanoi, Da Nang, Can Tho, and more. Get your order in 1–2 business days for urgent beauty needs.

Same-Day Delivery (HCMC only):

Order before 12:00 PM and receive your package the same day. (Available Mon–Sat, Districts 1–10, Bình Thạnh, Phú Nhuận, and Thủ Đức.)

🛍️ Shipping Fees & Free Shipping

  • Free standard shipping on all orders over 499,000₫
  • For orders below 499,000₫, a flat-rate shipping fee of 30,000₫–50,000₫ applies (varies by region)
  • Express and same-day shipping have additional charges, calculated at checkout

💳 Payment Options We support multiple secure and convenient payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) – Pay when your order arrives
  • Bank Transfer – Details provided at checkout
  • E-wallets – MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay
  • QR Code Pay – Scan and pay instantly via your preferred banking app

📍 Our Fulfillment Center All orders are shipped directly from our flagship store: Glow Haus – 2nd Floor, Vincom Center Đồng Khởi 📍 72 Lê Thánh Tôn, Bến Nghé Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City

Our packaging team ensures every item is sealed with care, wrapped in eco-conscious materials, and ready to impress.

📲 Order Tracking & Support Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email or Zalo. Need help? Our customer support team is available every day from 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM to assist you with:

  1. Tracking updates
  2. Shipping delays
  3. Address changes
  4. Delivery instructions
